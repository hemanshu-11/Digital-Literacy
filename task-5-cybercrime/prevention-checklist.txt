# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## For College Students in India

## General Cybersecurity Tips

1. Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Do not reuse passwords.

2. Enable two-factor authentication for all accounts, such as email, banking, and social media.

3. Do not click on suspicious links sent via SMS, WhatsApp, or email, even if they appear to be from trusted sources.

4. Be cautious of anyone who contacts you claiming to be from a bank or government agency.

5. Ensure your phone and apps are updated to avoid any security vulnerabilities.

6. Do not share your personal information like Aadhaar number, PAN, etc., with unknown websites.

## UPI and Financial Safety Tips

7. NEVER share your UPI PIN with anyone, not even bank officials. No honest individual would ask for your UPI PIN.

8. Remember, you are always SENDING money when you enter your UPI PIN. Be cautious of receiving money.

## Reporting Channels

- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7, All India)

- Report immediately if you are a victim. Early reporting has a good chance of getting back your money.
