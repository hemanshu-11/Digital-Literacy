## Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

### DO'S
1. Your profile must be professional and updated.
2. Use different and complex passwords.
3. Think before you post. Remember, once you post it online, it stays forever.
4. Follow educational and professional accounts.
5. Report cyberbullying at once.

### DON'TS
1. Do not share personal information such as address and phone number.
2. Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
3. Do not post negative comments about classmates and teachers.
4. Do not click on suspicious links and pop-up ads.
5. Do not use the same password for all accounts.
